# Tsundoku Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 20, 2026

Tsundoku does not collect, transmit to us, sell, share, or use your personal
data for tracking, advertising, analytics, or marketing.

## Data collection

The developer collects **no data** from this app. Tsundoku has no developer-run
analytics, advertising, telemetry, crash-reporting, or account service.

## Your servers and devices

Tsundoku connects only to the Komga and Kavita servers that you choose to add.
To provide reading features, the app communicates directly with those servers
using the credentials you provide. This may include requests for your library,
covers, book content, downloads, and reading-progress updates. Those requests
are governed by the privacy practices of the server operator.

The app stores its settings, credentials, library cache, downloads, bookmarks,
and reading progress on your device. When you enable iCloud, Apple may
synchronize the app data designed for iCloud/Keychain synchronization across
your devices using your Apple account. The developer does not receive that
data.

Optional AniList and MyAnimeList integrations connect directly to those
services only when you choose to configure and use them. Their privacy policies
apply to those services.

## Children

Tsundoku does not collect personal information from anyone, including
children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in this file with
a new effective date.
